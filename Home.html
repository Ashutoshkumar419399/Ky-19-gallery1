Kashiyatra, the annual cultural festival of IIT(BHU), Varanasi is a three day fiesta, aimed towards mesmerizing everyone with enchanting literary, musical and artistic events. Its grandeur has earned it the fame of being North India's largest cultural festival. IIT-BHU rests amidst the sanctuary of Asia's largest residential campus, BHU in the holy city of Varanasi. Varanasi, the eternal city, houses a plethora of traditions and has an ecstatic blend of cultures of myriad races. IIT-BHU has been nationally acclaimed for imparting technical knowledge in diverse fields and IIT-BHU will be century in a couple of years.
